# Eagle Info Repair Manager Business Solution Summary

Eagle Info Repair Manager is a complete business operations platform built around the real workflow of a repair business. It starts from customer intake and job registration, then carries each repair through diagnosis, technician assignment, quotation, approval, invoice, payment receipt, and final delivery documentation.

Customers can also track the progress of their repair jobs, giving the business a more transparent service experience from intake to completion. Job status, repair progress, communication updates, and handover readiness can be managed from within the system, reducing repeated calls and improving customer confidence.

The platform also supports client complaint tracking, allowing customer issues to be captured, followed up, managed, and tracked until resolution. This creates accountability around after-service support and helps management monitor unresolved complaints, service quality issues, and customer satisfaction risks.

At its core, the system manages repair jobs with full visibility for management and operations teams while protecting sensitive customer and pricing information from external technicians. Internal teams can track device details, client communication, diagnosis notes, repair progress, costs, approvals, payments, and job history in one place.

Beyond repair tracking, the platform supports the full commercial document flow: job cards, quotations, invoices, receipts, and delivery notes. This allows the business to move from service intake to billing and handover without relying on disconnected spreadsheets or manual paperwork.

The system also includes employee and technician performance management through job assignment tracking, audit history, technician workflows, payouts, follow-ups, and configurable user roles. Management can monitor who handled each job, what actions were taken, payment status, repair progress, and operational performance across the business.

For retail and front-desk operations, the platform includes POS support for quick sales, payments, receipts, refunds, credit notes, and inventory-linked sales. Inventory management supports parts tracking, stock movements, suppliers, purchase orders, and stock adjustments, helping the business control both repair parts and retail merchandise.

The result is a unified repair-management and business-control solution covering service operations, customer job tracking, complaint resolution, sales, inventory, staff accountability, customer communication, financial documents, audit trails, and management reporting from one secure system.
